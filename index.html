Visit Bearsden

Enjoy culture and sports in this beautiful suburb north of Glasgow.

Top three activities to do in Bearsden

Bearsden Ski Club

Try your hand at skiing or snowboarding at one of the best dry ski slopes in the world. Try freestyle, racing, tubing, or going of the big jump on a crash mat (WARNING: not an officialy sanctioned activity). Or just get pissed in the club house. The choice is yours!

New Kilpatrick Church

When there are no empty's to be had on a Friday night, come down to the big tree at the graveyard and smoke some weed with the crew. Feeling more adventurious? Try climbing the curch steeple instead! Who knows where the night might take you!

The Roman Baths

I can't recommend this place because I have no memory of ever actually being there. Although I know I probably went on a school trip at some point. That being said, Tripadvisor recons it' s a good place, and I'm not going to argue with the the internet!
